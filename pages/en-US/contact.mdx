# Contact Us

## Community Support

If you encounter any issues while using FastExcel, or have any suggestions, feel free to contact us through the following channels:

### GitHub

- Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/CodePhiliaX/fastexcel)
- Submit an [Issue](https://github.com/CodePhiliaX/fastexcel/issues)
- Participate in [Discussions](https://github.com/CodePhiliaX/fastexcel/discussions)

### Contribution Guidelines

If you would like to contribute to FastExcel, please refer to our [Contribution Guidelines](/zh-CN/docs/contribution).

### Stay Updated

Follow the WeChat official account of the author "Lazy Coder" for more technical content and the latest updates.
